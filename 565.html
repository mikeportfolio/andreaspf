Atlas from Geography 565



This is one of many maps that I created for my capstone project at UW - Madison.
My project specifically focused on the Black Lives Matter Movement and the various artwork that appeared throughout Madison, Wisconsin after the tragic death of George Floyd. I completed this project with Matthew McCalister, another UW Geography graduate, and our paper can be accessed through the Minds@UW website.
